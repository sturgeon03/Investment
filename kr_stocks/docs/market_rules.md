# Korea Market Rules

This document captures the market-specific assumptions that must be explicit before research begins.

## Execution Rules

- Use KRW as the base currency.
- Respect exchange-specific trading sessions.
- Apply realistic fees and tax assumptions for each security type.
- Model event timing at the filing or announcement level, not only at the calendar date level.

## Research Rules

- Keep universe history point-in-time if possible.
- Keep vendor assumptions separate from research logic.
- Avoid porting US-specific assumptions such as US settlement conventions or US tax treatment.
